Tips

  • Exceed any government recommendations and pressure them to increase those restrictions. You do not want to play chicken with an exponential graph.
  • Assume media coverage is sensationalizing the wrong things. This is simultaneously better and worse than they are reporting. Assume reporters are getting it wrong or asking stupid questions and seek underlying sources yourself.
  • Watch New York Governor Andrew Cuomo's press conferences on YouTube each morning. They are an excellent source of useful data and context that is insightful, direct, and sometimes darkly amusing.
  • Monitor Johns Hopkins worldwide case tracker dashboard for a current macro view.
  • Read this short Washington Post article that shows simple simulation animations of how different containment strategies play out
  • Read this long Medium article on why you must act now, understand Chart 7 in detail, learn how to use death rates to estimate spread, and learn how to convince people in power to act now.
